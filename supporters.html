Supporters

Lord Young of Norwood Green, former Skills Minister:

"I welcome the work being done by the Cross Industry Construction Apprenticeship Task Force to help ensure that the construction industry has the skills it needs for the future, and I support its aims"

Lord de Mauley:

“The work of the CCATF is invaluable given the current crisis facing apprenticeships in the construction industry. Its initiative means it deserves to succeed and I wish its members, too, every success.”

The CCATF membership is kept under close review to ensure there is representation from all sectors in the construction industry, as well as from the FE sector and government.


Close engagement with construction industry trade federations helps the CCATF to achieve its mission.
